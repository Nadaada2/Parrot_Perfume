Our Story

The pursuit of perfection in every drop.

Parrot Luxury was founded on the principle that fragrance is an intimate extension of the wearer. We reject the fleeting trends of mass production, choosing instead to focus on **timeless compositions** and **uncompromising quality**.

It began in Paris, 1985, with a singular vision: to distill the world's most evocative memories into enduring scents. Our founder, **Eleanor Dubois**, believed true elegance whispers, it does not shout.

We are committed to transparency. Our master perfumers travel the globe to source the rarest botanicalsâ€”from hand-picked Moroccan rose to sustainably harvested Indian sandalwood. Every ingredient is ethically sourced and meticulously verified.

Each collection is a quiet testament to the artistry and patience required to create true luxury. Our process is slow, deliberate, and personal. We age our concentrates for months to achieve perfect depth and complexity.
